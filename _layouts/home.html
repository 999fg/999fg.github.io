About Me

I am a Ph.D. Candidate in Networking & Mobile Systems Lab (NMSL) at KAIST, advised by Prof. Sung-Ju Lee. I am currently working as a research intern at Cisco Research with my mentor, Dr. Myungjin Lee from March 2023. Previously, I worked as a research intern in Heterogeneous and Extreme Computing (HEX) group at Microsoft Research Asia (MSRA) from December 2020 to June 2021, mentored by Dr. Yuanchun Li and Dr. Yunxin Liu. I received my B.S. degree in Computer Science from KAIST in 2018.

I work on the intersection of Federated Learning (FL), mobile healthcare (mHealth), and systems. I believe that FL is an essential tool to uncover novel mHealth technologies, and I focus on promoting active FL usage in mHealth application development through practical, system-based solutions.
