About me

I am a Ph.D. student in Networking & Mobile Systems Lab (NMSL) at KAIST, advised by Prof. Sung-Ju Lee. My research interest lies in developing and facilitating mobile and wearable AI systems for human health and well-being.

I received my B.S. degree in Computer Science from KAIST in 2018. I worked as a research intern in Systems and Networking Research Group at Microsoft Research Asia (MSRA) from December 2020 to June 2021, mentored by Dr. Yuanchun Li and Dr. Yunxin Liu. At MSRA, I worked on a project that proposes a systematic approach for efficient federated learning on heterogeneous mobile clients.
